Hi, I'm Ronald Lokers. **Loki Labs** = *Lokers* + the lab where I break and
rebuild things: a homelab, AI tooling, research experiments. This site is the
lab notebook.

## The short version

Over 15 years of frontend and fullstack development — at
[Coolblue](https://www.coolblue.nl), [Mendix](https://www.mendix.com), and
Oliver IT — taught me one thing above all: the part of the job I kept
gravitating toward was never the UI. It was the build tools, the CI/CD
pipelines, and the infrastructure underneath.

A health-related career break gave me the room to be honest about that.
(Fully under control — cleared and eager to get back to work.) So since
mid-2025 I've committed to the switch: platform engineering, full time.

## What that looks like in practice

- A multi-node Kubernetes cluster on Raspberry Pi CM5 hardware — three HA
  control planes — that I run like production: GitOps with FluxCD, every piece
  of infrastructure declared in Git ([repo](https://github.com/ronaldlokers/homelab))
- Full observability: Prometheus, Grafana, dashboards, alerting
- Longhorn storage, MetalLB load balancing, Renovate keeping everything current
- Daily drivers: Linux (Arch), Docker, CI/CD pipeline design, cluster operations

`kubernetes · fluxcd · gitops · docker · prometheus · grafana · linux · ci/cd · typescript`

Ready to bring 15+ years of engineering experience to a Platform or DevOps
role. [Get in touch](mailto:ronald@lokilabs.nl).
