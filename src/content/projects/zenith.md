Every application, company, and recruiter contact in one place — because a
job search *is* a pipeline, and pipelines deserve dashboards.

- Kanban board across five stages with drag-to-restage, filters, saved views
- Sourced-listings inbox: pulls fresh roles every 6 hours from Adzuna and
  Greenhouse/Ashby boards, filtered by role keywords — swipe to triage
- CV builder with multi-language support and PDF export
- Analytics: conversion funnel, response rate, median time-in-stage, ghost rate

Mobile-first, privacy-respecting, no analytics or tracking. Runs entirely on
Cloudflare's free tier. Invite-only. Built with Claude Code.
